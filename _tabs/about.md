I'm a security enthusiast who likes to actually get hands dirty rather than
just read about things. This blog is where I track what I'm learning as I
go — half notes-to-self, half write-ups, all still very much a work in
progress. Open threads, appropriately.

I dabble in a bit of everything: building out a home lab, working through
practical exercises, and slowly making my way toward bug bounty work. I'm
also picking up new tools and platforms along the way, sometimes well
outside security itself.

I'm not writing as an expert — I'm writing as I learn. If something here is
wrong or incomplete, that's honestly the most useful comment you could
leave me.
